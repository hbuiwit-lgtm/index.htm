📋 Student Info

1
🦶 Digital Footprint

Meet Your Influencer

1.1 What platforms is this influencer active on? Check all that apply.
1.2 Describe the type of content this influencer mostly posts. What is their niche or topic?
Think: gaming, beauty, cooking, sports commentary, comedy, education, etc.
1.3 How long do you think this influencer has been creating content online? What clues helped you figure this out?
Look at post dates, follower counts, early vs. recent videos, etc.
2
🦶 Digital Footprint

Footprint Deep Dive

2.1 List 3 pieces of personal information this influencer has publicly shared online (e.g. their city, school, real name, family members, daily routine).
Be specific — note where you found each piece of info.
2.2 Do you think this influencer is careful about what personal information they share? Rate it:
2.3 If you searched this influencer's name 5 years from now, what kinds of posts or content do you think would still show up? Why does that matter?
3
🪞 Online Identity

Who Are They Online?

3.1 In 3–5 words, how would you describe this influencer's online persona — the version of themselves they show to the world?
Example: "funny, relatable, fashion-focused, motivational"
3.2 Do you think their online identity matches who they really are in real life? Explain your thinking.
Consider: Do they ever "break character"? Share personal struggles? Seem authentic?
3.3 Think about the values this influencer seems to represent. What messages do they send — on purpose or accidentally — to their audience?
3.4 Has this influencer ever posted something that got them in trouble (drama, controversy, backlash)? If yes, describe it briefly. If no, describe a risk you think they could face.

Your Verdict
